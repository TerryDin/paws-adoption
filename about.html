About Paws Adoption

Our mission is to find loving forever homes for every pet that comes through our doors.

Our Mission

At Paws Adoption, we believe every animal deserves a second chance. We're dedicated to rescuing, rehabilitating, and rehoming pets in need. Through compassionate care, thorough adoption screening, and community education, we create lasting bonds between pets and the people who love them.

Our Story

Paws Adoption was founded in 2016 by a small group of animal lovers who saw a pressing need in our community. Every year, thousands of pets were being surrendered to overcrowded shelters with limited resources. Our founders knew they had to do something.

Starting with just a handful of foster homes and a lot of determination, we began rescuing pets from high-kill shelters and placing them in loving foster families. The first year, we found homes for 47 pets. The word spread quickly, and our network grew.

By 2019, we had expanded into our own facility —a warm, welcoming space where pets could stay while waiting for their forever families. Our adoption process focuses on matching the right pet with the right family, ensuring that every adoption is a successful, lifelong commitment.

Today, with over 500 successful adoptions and a dedicated team of staff and volunteers, we continue our mission with the same passion that started it all. Every pet that walks through our doors leaves with a second chance at happiness.
